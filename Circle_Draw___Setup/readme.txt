Special Instructions: There are no special instructions for the normal operation of the program. Outlined below are the special features and their functions: * Pressing and holding the “x” key will make all the particles travel in the y-direction (up and down).* Pressing and holding the “y” key will make all the particles travel in the x-direction (left and right).* Pressing and holding the “f” key will freeze the animation. * Pressing the “r” key will reset the animation.
