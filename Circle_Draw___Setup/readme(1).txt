Special Instructions: 

There are no special instructions for the normal operation of the program. Outlined below are the functions of the included extra features: 

* Pressing and holding the “x” key will make all the particles travel in the y-direction (up and down).
* Pressing and holding the “y” key will make all the particles travel in the x-direction (left and right).
* Pressing the “r” key will reverse the direction of the particles. 
* Pressing and holding the “r” key will freeze the animation.
